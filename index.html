About Us

Fajibs Cakez Edible Solutions Limited (also trading as Fajibs Cakez Foodservice Tech Co) represents the convergence of culinary excellence and technological innovation. Founded and managed by an experienced Poultry Tech Manager, we understand the intricate balance between food safety, quality production, and scalable technology solutions.

Our facility in Umuahia North serves as the hub for our diverse operations – from creating delectable cakes and pastries that grace celebrations across Abia State, to providing comprehensive poultry technology management and foodservice solutions for businesses looking to modernize their operations.

We don't just follow food trends; we create them. Our commitment to innovation means constantly exploring new techniques, ingredients, and technologies that enhance food production, preservation, and delivery. Whether you're a customer seeking premium baked goods or a business partner looking for foodtech solutions, Fajibs Cakez delivers excellence at every touchpoint.
